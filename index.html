Rules & Important Notes
  1. Check In and Check Out
    • Check in Timings for all bookings is 1pm
    • Check Out Timings for all bookings is 11am
  2. Registration & Identification
    • All guests must provide a valid government-issued photo ID at check-in.
    • Only registered guests are permitted in the guest rooms. Unregistered visitors are strictly prohibited from staying overnight.
  3. Payments & Billing
    • All accommodation bills must be settled in full upon or prior to departure, depending on hotel policy.
  4. Safety & Security
    • Use the in-room cupboards. The House is typically not liable for lost or stolen valuables not secured with employees.
    • Hazardous materials, weapons, and illegal drugs are strictly prohibited on the premises.
    • Always utilize the deadbolt or safety latch when inside your room.
  5. Code of Conduct
    • Usually quiet Hours are recommended between 10 PM and 8 AM. Disruptive behavior, gambling, and loud noise that disturbs other guests are not tolerated.
    • Smoking and vaping are strictly prohibited inside all enclosed House spaces and rooms, with smoking areas located outdoors.
    • Guests are held financially liable for any damage caused to the room or hotel property.
  6. Housekeeping & Amenities
    • 🚫 No access to the swimming pool on the next morning(checkout day)
    • Guests are responsible for maintaining the cleanliness of their rooms and should report any maintenance issues promptly.
    • Housekeeping services generally operate between set morning and afternoon hours. "Do Not Disturb" signs are respected.
    • Pets are strictly prohibited unless the property explicitly allows them.
